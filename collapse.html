It is an application that provides the location and information in real time of all the great white sharks discovered and monitored up to now.

Our team breaks the conventions and bad habits of territorial scientific investigations that disrupt the peace of the ocean. Promoting educational promulgation and contemporary revolution.

Together with a beautiful team of 400 professionals and collaborators, we have carried out 44 expeditions, 90 institutions and published 75 documents. All material is available to everyone in the app.
